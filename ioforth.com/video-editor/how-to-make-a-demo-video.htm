Wednesday December 30, 2020 10:24 PM PDT by Karen Greene

How to Make A Demo Video

To make a demo video can give you the space to show rather than tell consumers how it will change their life. Cheaper than a sales team, an online demo also broadens your reach, and you can analyze if or at which point your visitors/viewers drop off.

Fledgling startups shouldn’t be deterred either by lack of budget or experience in the filmmaking world. There is plenty of easy-to-use technology and applications that make the process of demonstrating products on video straightforward and a whole lot of fun.

The following guide takes you through, step-by-step, everything you need to do to make your product demo, including planning, recording, and editing your clips for a seamless output.

It finishes with a cheat sheet of how to create a product demo to pack a punch.

Step 1. Planning How to Create Product Demo Video Content

If you’re used to delivering on the fly, you may want to rethink that strategy for your product demo video. Instead, a carefully constructed plan will help you craft a video that achieves your goals. Here’s what you should consider.

  • What’s the goal? Are you trying to boost sales or subscriptions?
  • Who’s the audience? Do you want customers to return, or are you trying to reach new markets?
  • Identify the content. Do you want to answer common questions or explain features?
  • Layout the format. Write down or create a storyboard of how your product demo will be structured.

Step 2. Making the Product Demo Video

This step depends on whether your product is physical or non-physical like software. How to create demo video content for physical products.

Always use a tripod to keep the camera steady and to avoid shake. A microphone also helps with sound quality while a source of light will minimize weird shadows.

Step 3. Now share your final product demo

Now that you’ve done the work of creating a product demo video, you can share it with your target audience. Social media provides obvious platforms for this if you have relevant channels set up. You can also embed the video on your website or within an email.

Product Demo Videos for Impact – Cheat Sheet

Now you know creating product demo video content isn’t difficult; how can you do this with impact?

  • Think benefits rather than features – how will this product better the consumer’s life or solve their problems.
  • Demonstrate how the product works, step-by-step, without too much detail.
  • End with a call-to-action. That’s where you go back to why you’re making the video in the first place. Do you want them to buy, give you their email, or try your product for a trial period?
  • Keep it short and simple. You’re competing with tons of content out there, and attention spans are limited. So, keep the length of the video as short as possible while allowing for an adequate demonstration.
