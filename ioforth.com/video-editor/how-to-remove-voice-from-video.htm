How to Remove Voice from Video

Thursday November 19, 2020 8:31 PM PDT by Karen Greene

There are several reasons why anyone would want to learn how to remove voice from video clips. Perhaps, you shot a great video on a fun day, but the noise in the background kept ruining it for you. Or, maybe you simply want to do a clean voiceover on an existing video and need to keep the film completely quiet to effectively get your message across.

Thanks to 20th-century technology, it is pretty easy to remove voice from video clips, and in this tutorial, you will learn how to get rid of annoying sounds from your videos in less than 5 minutes using your Windows 10 PC and mobile devices!

How to Remove Audio from Video in Windows 10

Ever since the removal of Windows Movie Maker, people are finding it hard getting used to the new Microsoft Photos, which has become a replacement for the discontinued video editor.

Nonetheless, Microsoft Photos can be used to do quite a few things, including getting rid of sound from your videos.

So many people can’t figure it out, but in this section, I’ll show you how it’s done. Let’s jump into it!

Step 1: Open the Microsoft Photos App

If you can’t find your Microsoft Photos app right away, simply tap on the Windows Icon on the bottom left corner of your screen and start typing “Photos,” the Microsoft Photos icon will show up. Click on it, and you’ll be taken into the app.

Done? Great!

Now, it’s time for the real stuff.

Step 2: Select Video Editor and Start a New Video Project

At the top right corner of the Microsoft Photos app, you will see an array of options: Collections, Albums, People, Folders, and Video Editor.

Select a video editor. On the new window, select New Video Project.

Select New Video Project

When you click on New Video Project, you’ll be prompted to input a file name. You can choose to add a file name or simply select skip.

Step 3: Add your Video

First, click on Add. If you have stored your videos on your PC, select From this PC, navigate to where you’ve stored your video, and select it.

Project Library

The video will show up in the Project Library section. Now it’s time to remove the sound from your video.

Step 4: Remove the Sound

Drag the video from the Project Library to the Storyboard section.

Remove the Sound

Next, tap on the sound icon on the video thumbnail twice. This will mute the sound of the audio.
Mission completed!

You can now proceed with saving the new video.

To be fair, using Microsoft Photos for removing voice from videos is not easy for most people. So if you’d like to do this way faster and easier, we have a solution for you.

However, they mostly work the same way:

The name says it all. Mute Video is very simple and allows you to quickly remove sound from your video without compromising the quality. Apart from just muting your video, the app also gives you the option to compress your video before export.
