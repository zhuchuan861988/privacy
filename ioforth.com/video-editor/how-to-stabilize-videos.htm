Stabilize a Video

Are you looking to remove shake from the video? There can be plenty of software to help create smooth footage. But, only a few software like FilmForth stand out.

Each footage has some degree of shakes. That makes it unprofessional and difficult to watch. Besides, it can trigger a motion-sickness response in some viewers.

How FilmForth Stabilizes Videos?

Shaky footage isn’t appealing. But avoiding them is almost impossible. FilmForth can be the right software to remove shake from the video. Here is the procedure:

Step 1: Click on the Stabilize Video icon.

FilmForth-Stabilize Video

Step 2: Next, click on Select a Video File and then open the video clip in FilmForth.

Step 3: Give the video file an output name. After that, choose the folder to save the video files.

FilmForth – Save Video

Now, there comes an optional step. Before you close the Stabilize Video screen, you can click Show in Folder. It is an action that opens Windows Explorer at the video file location.
