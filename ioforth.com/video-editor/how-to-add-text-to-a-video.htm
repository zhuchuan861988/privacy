How to Add Text to A Video with FilmForth

Sunday March 7, 2021 12:54 PM PDT by Karen Greene

Video consumption over the years has skyrocketed. YouTube, Netflix, and social media are the primary players behind the rally. Video content is consumed across different devices such as phones, TVs, tablets, and more. And around ⅓ of the online activity is spent watching videos.

The content is published in dozens of different languages. Some are difficult to comprehend due to linguistic differences. On the other hand, the audio might not be the best of a few.

It is best to add a caption, subtitle over the video. That will provide clarity and help users understand the undertone message which is being conveyed.

How to Add Text to a Video via Free Video Editor

Putting text over a video does not require any shenanigans or complicated processes, to begin with. You will need easy-going software that does the job well without requiring you to scratch your head.

Video editors such as FilmForth make it incredibly easy to add text to video for free. No need to spend hundreds of dollars when the free solution is right there in front of you. In the next part of this post, we will introduce how to use FilmForth to add ext to a video step by step. Go on reading and figure them out.

Step1. Download FilmForth on a computer.

It’s currently available for Microsoft’s latest installment.

Launch FilmForth from the taskbar or the desktop shortcut. The home screen of the video editor will appear.

How to Add Text to Video for Free

Click on the New Project button to create a fresh project for the new video, which needs text on it. You can also add text to an old video that you have worked on in the editor. The project will appear on the home screen as the recent projects.

Give your project a name and set the location to save it. The video will be saved else, as we will learn in the later step.

Create a New Project via FilmForth

Step 2. Add A Video to FilmForth.

Now, it’s time to add the video in which you wish to add the text. You can either drop and drop the video into the video or manually browse it.

Add Your Project

Step 3. Add Text to the Video.

Click on the Text option from the dashboard. Alternatively, you can click on the icon from the video feed as well.

Add Text on Your Video

After that, click on the Add Text button, accompanied by a plus button.

Add or Edit Text on Video

A textbox will appear on the video. Add the text of choice along with the option of over a dozen fonts. Different colors, alignment, and sizes are also available.

Edit Text to Video

You can determine the length of time for which a particular text will appear on the video. Click on the handlebars of a specific feed of text and resize it according to your needs.

The other way is to use the Set Start and Set End buttons. Seek your video.

Check Your Duration and Offset

Step 4. Save the Video.

Once all the relevant texts have been added to the video, click on the Save Video button. Go through the basic frame rates, resolution, and you are done.
