Frequently Asked Questions

What's Filmforth?

FilmForth is an easy and powerful video editor for Windows 10 users. It enables you to edit, trim, crop, add text, audio, pic in pic, and effects to empower your creativity.

Is FilmForth Free?

Yes, FilmForth is completely free to use. It allows users to enjoy all the free features from the application. You can get it from the Microsoft Store.

Does FilmForth have a watermark?

By default, users will find the words “Created with FilmForth” on the bottom left when exporting video objects. As a free product, we hope more users will know FilmForth and strengthen our brand influences. We would be very grateful if users keep the watermark.

However, you can remove the watermark by unchecking the “Export with a watermark”. For more details, please check the guide.

Is FilmForth safe to use?

Yes, it is truly safe and reliable. FilmForth is approved and certified by the Microsoft Store. Users can access the application on the Microsoft Store without undue fear of viruses, malware, or unauthorized attacks. It enables you to use all the security and distinctive features.

How to install FilmForth?

1. Go to www.ioforth.com/filmforth/.

2. Click the [Download] button at the upper right of the Home page.

3. Click the [Get] button.

4. You may need to address a dialogue to allow Microsoft Store to handle the Get link. Click [Allow] to open your Microsoft Store app locally.

5. Click the [Install] button when the Microsoft Store is open. Once the program is installed, confirm it and [Launch].

How to add a photo or video clip to FilmForth?

Please follow the detailed steps below to add it.

1. Open the project to add pictures or videos in FilmForth.

2. Click the Add Object [+] button to the right on the Timeline display.

3. Navigate to the storage location of the object you want to incorporate into your video.

4. Click on the object to highlight it in the file panel.

5. Click [Open]. The object will be added to the project timeline immediately after the last object in the timeline.

How to add music with FilmForth?

Please follow the detailed steps below to add music.

1. Open a project that already has graphic objects on the timeline, and click the Audio feature on the bottom bar of the project screen.

2. Click the Add Audio [+] icon to the left on the function bar display. The timeline panel will display two areas. To the left will be a Suggested Music panel which has a selection of pre-recorded songs that are free to use. To the right is the Import Audio panel.

3. To add voiceovers, use FilmForth’s Voiceover feature to record them directly.

How to add text in FilmForth?

Please follow the steps below to add text.

1. Move the yellow playhead in the Timeline to the position where you want to insert the text in the project.

2. Click on the [Text] icon on the toolbar at the bottom of the FilmForth screen.

3. Click [Add Text] at the left of the toolbar.

4. Click the [Edit Text] button to the right of [Add Text].

5. Enter the desired text in the text box.

If I delete and reinstall the app, will I lose my projects?

You will not lose your projects edited before. Please go to This PC -> videos -> FilmForth -> Projects and you can find all the video projects.

How can I change the aspect ratio with FilmForth?

1. Open the project in FilmForth.

2. Click the [Project Settings] button and you will find the Project Settings screen.

3. Click the Aspect Ratio icon and change the aspect ratio as you desired.

What should I do if the exported video quality is poor?

FilmForth allows users to adjust output settings. Video quality is closely related to Video Resolution, Frame Rate, and Video Bitrate. It is recommended to keep these parameters the same as the original video.
