How to Record YouTube Audio on PC

Saturday March 20, 2021 4:28 PM PDT by Karen Greene

YouTube is said to be the archiver of the videos of the Internet. If it’s on the Internet, the chances are that it can be found on the video-sharing platform. Some pieces of old music, podcasts, and audiobooks, amongst other things, can be found only on YouTube. Even Spotify takes a backseat at times when it comes to the obscure collection.

Recording YouTube audio is a straightforward process; depends on whom you ask. You can use online tools, as well as some applications meant for Windows.

Let’s start with online tools that are easiest for recording YouTube audio.

#1. KeepVid

KeepVid has been around for a long time. Enter the YouTube URL, and it will show several options to choose from for downloading the audio or the video.

How to Record Audio from YouTube

Features

  • Different audio formats

You will find several audio formats such as MP3, M4A, WEBM, amongst others, in 320kbps bitrate.

  • Bookmarklet

Bookmark the short javascript code, and it will act as a shortcut to download any audio from YouTube. When you press the Bookmarklet from your bookmarks on a YouTube page, it will take you to KeepVid for downloading the video/audio of that YouTube video.

  • Dedicated App

A dedicated app of KeepVid is also available, which is available for Android and Windows.

Pros

  • Multiple audio formats
  • Shortcut available via Bookmarklet
  • Audio can be downloaded from multiple websites

Cons

  • Only one bitrate of audio can be downloaded
  • Allows only audios of 30 mins or shorter

Steps to Download the Audio via KeepVid

Step 1. Head to the website and enter the video’s URL in the box.

Step 2. Press Go, and the results will appear below.

Step 3. Head to the audio section and click on the download button of MP3.

#2. YT1s

It’s all about keeping it swift and clear with YT1s. The download process is easier than KeepVid with its limited options.

YT1s Downloader

Features

  • Straightforward interface

No frills and extra features. Capture audio from YouTube video without any hassle.

  • No Audio length limit

Download audio of length from a YouTube video. There are no caps on that.

  • Cloud Support

Capture YouTube audio and upload it directly to cloud services – Google Drive and Dropbox.

Pros

  • Easy-to-use UI.
  • No limit on downloads or audio length.
  • Sharing option to social media.

Cons

  • Only MP3 format available.
  • Intrusive ads.

Steps to Download the Audio via YT1s

Step 1. Paste the YouTube link in the box and press the Convert button.

Step 2. Select MP3 format from the dropdown menu.

Step 3. Click on the Get Link button.

Step 4. Now click on the Download button.

#3. Ddownr

Ddownr is a rather underrated tool to capture YouTube audio. Similar to YT1s, it offers a toned-down and simpler version.

Ddownr

Features

  • Shareable download link (deep links)

Create a shareable link that can be used to download the audio of a YouTube video.

  • No Ads

No advertisements on the website’s page.

  • Direct Download button

Ddownr offers a one-click download of YouTube content.

Pros

  • Faster download process.
  • Offer two audio formats – MP3 and M4A.
  • Quick conversion to audio.

Cons

  • No batch download
  • Intrusive ads

Steps to Download the Audio via Ddownr

Step 1. Paste the YouTube link in the URL box.

Step 2. Click on Download and select the audio format.

Step 3. Click on the download button.

#4. Free Download Manager

FDM does the job of a download manager as well as downloading YouTube video/audio. It is available for Windows, Linux, Mac, and Android.

Free Download Manager

Features

  • Instant conversion to audio

Select the audio while downloading, and it will start downloading right away.

  • Local download

The audio is downloaded directly to your local storage of the computer.

  • Fastest Download Speeds

Being a download manager, it downloads YouTube audio at the fastest pace.

Pros

  • No ads anywhere.
  • The audio download can be paused.
  • Fastest conversion to audio.

Cons

  • No MP3 format.
  • Extra steps for downloading.

Steps to Download the Audio via Free Download Manager

Step 1. Open FDM and click on the plus button.

Step 2. Paste the URL and press OK.

Step 3. Select the format as M4A or WEB in the audio section.

Step 4. Click on Download.
