For example, it is easier to watch a video tutorial than read it. Videos are also a great way to share content and great ideas.

So, if you are planning on sharing great content on YouTube, you are on the right track and we are here to make your journey easier by sharing the 12 best video editing software for YouTube.

Our list would contain the best free and paid video editing software for both YouTube beginners and Pros. Let’s get right into it:

#3. iMovie

iMovie Overview

iMovie comes free with all Apple devices and has long been the first port-of-call for anyone new to video editing and is recommended for YouTubers. With easy-to-use features like splitting and cutting, iMovie allows you to produce videos like you earned an editing degree. Some of its other outstanding features include:

  • Allowing you to fade audios in and out.
  • Picture-in-picture feature.
  • Split and trim video clips.
  • A wide array of filters, text motion graphics, and themes.
  • Compatibility with popular video formats.

PROS

  • You can upload directly to Social Media.
  • Transition speed.

CONS

  • Movies can no longer be edited when it is exported.
  • Not enough features for motion tracking.

  • Supports a wide range of video formats.

  • VR rotates the sphere.

PROS

  • It has a lot of customizable features and options.

CONS

  • It is not free, an official license costs a lot.
  • The interface can be really tricky since it contains a lot of features.
