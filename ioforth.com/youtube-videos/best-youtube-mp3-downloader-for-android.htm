10 Best YouTube Mp3 Downloaders for Android Phone

Friday February 26, 2021 6:14 PM PDT by Karen Greene

Over a billion users visit YouTube every month for listening to music, shared by Elias Roman, product manager at YouTube, in a blog post.

Many people have started downloading their favorite music videos to save on data. Post-2018 downloading YouTube videos have become an everyday trick now due to various tools and apps offering the service for our android phones.

Users have gone one step further and now prefer MP3s for adding to their offline playlist. It helps save on the expensive mobile data and a quicker way to relish your favorite music on the go.

There are several online tools and apps that let you convert YouTube videos into MP3 files. That’s not all; some of them even let you search for videos and convert them from inside their android app as well.
