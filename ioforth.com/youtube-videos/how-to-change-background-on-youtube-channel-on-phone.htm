How to Change Background on YouTube Channel on Phone

Make your channel stand out by changing your YouTube channel’s background picture. Also known as the channel cover picture among many. It is not possible through the official YouTube app for mobiles. Even the YouTube Studio app for mobiles does no good in this matter.

Step 1.Open YouTube on your browser.

Google Chrome should work just fine. Make sure to be logged in to your Google account beforehand.

Step 2. Tap on the three vertical dots once YouTube has loaded on your mobile browser.

Change Background on YouTube Channel on Phone

Step 3.  A menu will appear, scroll down a notch. Tap on the “Desktop Mode”, and the page will be refreshed automatically.

The desktop version of the website will load up.

Choose the Desktop Mode

Step 4. Head to Studio.Youtube.com and on the screen’s left side, you will find a vertical bar with several menus. Then find and tap on the magic wand, which is second from the bottom which will take you to the Channel Customization page.

Click the Magic Wind Button

Step 5. There will be three headers. Tap on Branding to access that section, which is denoted by arrow A. Tap on change under the Banner Image header, denoted by arrow B, and select the photo of your choice.

Also, if it’s your first time adding a YouTube background banner, you will see there is an UPLOAD on the arrow B. Just the same step you have followed will help you to figure out how to add background image to youtube channel.

How to Change YouTube Channel Background

Step 6. Once you have selected the image, click on the Publish button, as denoted by the arrow C in the above screenshot.

Take into account that only specific dimensions will be visible in the background picture. The prompt will guide you on ways to maximize visibility on different devices.

Once you have changed your YouTube background banner successfully, you could go to your YuTube app on the phone. Pull to refresh the app and then you will see the new background banner.

Segment A – Detach audio and Add Background Music

Audio may not have been captured well on your camera. It’s better to detach it and add music, audio of your own.

As we are wrapped up with the audio section, let’s move to the visual part of the video. Default color-grading of a video is often monotonous and does not set the needed overtone for a video.
