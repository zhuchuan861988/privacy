How to Record YouTube Videos?

Tuesday March 23, 2021 10:48 PM PDT by Karen Greene

Undeniably, YouTube is a popular video platform offering good entertainment to viewers. Mainly, viewers download the videos to watch later or share with friends. It’s a friendly method, but it requires you to have a YouTube downloader. Nevertheless, there is one more way of getting YouTube videos – recording them to your device.

With the right software, you can easily record YouTube videos on iPhone, PC, Mac, or even android phone. This write-up shows you how you can record any YouTube video on the devices named above. What’s more, we will also reveal an excellent method to edit your recorded videos.

Record YouTube Videos

By now, you know that you can record YouTube videos on devices without a YouTube downloader. This fantastic development allows you to enjoy videos on any device, and the process is pretty straightforward. Undoubtedly, this is a handy method for most YouTube video lovers.

Now, let’s explore four possibilities to record YouTube videos.

How to Record YouTube Videos on PC?

AceThinker

So, you want to know how to record YouTube Videos on a PC? Well, the best way is to record that video online using an online YouTube video recorder like AceThinker for your PC or Mac.

AceThinker is web-based, meaning you’ll be using it straight on the web instead of downloading screencast software to your computer.

Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Head over to YouTube and identify the video you want to download. Open the online video recording tool and then click “Start Recording.” This action launches the free online screen recorder.

Step 2: Check the recording settings and confirm them before beginning to record your videos.

Step 3: When the recording is complete, stop and save your recording to your computer or upload it online.

How to Record YouTube Videos on Mac?

QuickTime Player

Recording a youtube video on Mac is straightforward, as you’ll see in this step-by-step guide on how to record YouTube videos on Mac using QuickTime Player.

It is an excellent method for creating a montage, and it’s faster. Besides, it is excellent at capturing sections of a whole video. But, it may not result in high-quality video or sound.

Steps to follow:

Step 1: Turn off programs that might generate sounds, e.g., email.

Step 2: Head over to the browser and open a YouTube clip. Adjust the size of the YouTube player to play a large size – use the Theatre mode. Then adjust the volume of the Mac to at least 80%.

Step 3: Identify a point you’d wish to begin recording and pause. For better recording, go back 3 seconds.

Step 4: Open QuickTime Player and click on the menu> File>New Screen Recording. A black dialogue box with the inscription “Screen Recording” pops up. Get that box out of the frame.

Step 5: Click on the downward pointing V (to the right of the red record button on the Screen Recording), and from the pull-down menu, select Internal Microphone.

Step 6: Draw a selection around the video box by clicking and holding your mouse. It is an action that cuts out the section. Click the Start Recording button, followed by the play button on the YouTube video. Allow the video to play up to the end of the section you want to capture and then pause.

Step 7: Stop the QuickTime Recording by clicking the black circle at the top-right menu.

Step 8: Name and save your video clip in the pop box. Play it to determine the sound and video quality.

How to Record YouTube Videos on iPhone?

iOS 11 has a screen recording feature, making it easy to capture YouTube videos. The following steps are pretty clear on how to record YouTube videos on iPhone.

Step 1: Head down to YouTube and find the video you wish to record. Set your iPhone to play the video in landscape.

Step 2: Now, go to the Control Centre. Once there, swipe up, and then tap on the record button to begin recording the video. When it’s done, stop recording – Head over to Photos, i.e., point of storage of the recording. Check it out to ascertain quality.

Record Button in iPhone

If you are using iOS versions earlier than iOS 11, use QuickTime on your Mac.

Step 1: Open QuickTime on your Mac and use the menu to choose New Movie Recording.

Step 2: Plug your iPhone into your Mac and tap trust when prompted (on the iPhone).

Step 3: In QuickTime, select your iPhone by clicking on the small arrow to the right of the record button.

How to Record YouTube Videos on Android Phone?

Record YouTube Video on Android Phone

Some Android smartphones come with inbuilt screen recording features. All phones with updated android 10 of Samsung, Vivo, OPPO, and LG brand have this feature.

Thus, follow the steps below to learn how to record YouTube videos on an Android phone, specifically Samsung Galaxy.

Step 1: Head over to YouTube and play the video of your choice. Swipe down from the top twice to fully expand the “Quick Settings” menu.

Step 2: Select “Screen recorder.” Sometimes you may not find it. So, swipe left to get to the next page of tiles – 2nd or 3rd.

The device asks if you want to record audio. Thus, you can choose to record media, media sounds, and the microphone, or no audio.

Step 3: A countdown starts, and upon reaching zero, it initiates the process of video recording. Stop the recording process whenever you want.
